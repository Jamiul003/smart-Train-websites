About SmartTrain

An innovative mobile application designed to provide real-time information on train routes, schedules, and live locations for railway passengers in Bangladesh. Our primary goal is to ensure a smooth and informative travel experience for all users.

Our Core Objectives

  • Provide reliable, real-time train information.
  • Create a hybrid data model using user and official data.
  • Reduce passenger inconvenience and fill information gaps.

For App Users

  • Access accurate, live train data to save time.
  • Plan journeys efficiently with reliable information.

  • Receive emergency alerts for enhanced safety.

  • Enjoy a user-friendly, on-the-go experience.

For Bangladesh Railway

  • Automate information to improve operational efficiency.
  • Gather real-time data for better decision-making.
  • Modernize services and enhance public trust.
  • Explore new revenue streams and optimize resources.
